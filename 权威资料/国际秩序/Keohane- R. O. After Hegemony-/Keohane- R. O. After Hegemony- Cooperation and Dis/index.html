    Keohane, R. O. After Hegemony: Cooperation and Discord in the World Political Economy[M]. Princeton: Princeton University Press, 1984.¶

    Keohane, R. O. After Hegemony：Cooperation and Discord in the World Political Economy[M]. Princeton：Princeton University Press, 1984.

    Robert O. Keohane （罗伯特·基欧汉）是当代国际关系理论中最具影响力的学者之一，新自由制度主义（Neoliberal Institutionalism）的奠基者和代表人物。他长期致力于研究国际合作、国际制度、国际政治经济学与全球治理，对“在无政府国际体系中如何实现合作”这一核心问题作出了系统性理论回应。

    在理论贡献上，Keohane 最重要的创新在于突破了现实主义“合作依赖霸权”的判断。他在代表作After Hegemony（1984）中提出，即便在缺乏单一霸权国家的情况下，国际制度仍能通过降低交易成本、提供信息、减少不确定性和约束机会主义行为，促进国家间持续合作。这一论断系统回应了现实主义对国际合作可持续性的怀疑，成为国际制度研究的经典范式。

    在学术谱系上，Keohane 早期与 Joseph Nye 合作提出“复杂相互依存”理论，强调国家间关系已不再完全由军事安全主导，而是受到经济、制度和跨国网络的深刻影响；中后期则将研究重心转向制度如何塑造国家行为，推动国际关系研究从“权力—安全”视角拓展至“规则—治理”视角。

    《After Hegemony》核心观点原文表述与对应页码（Robert O. Keohane，1984年版）¶

    罗伯特·基欧汉（Robert O. Keohane）在《霸权之后：世界政治经济中的合作与纷争》（After Hegemony: Cooperation and Discord in the World Political Economy）中，以“霸权衰落”为背景，核心探讨“无霸权状态下国际合作何以可能”，通过“国际机制（International Regimes）”理论、理性选择分析与制度功能主义，重构了国际政治经济学的合作逻辑。以下为核心观点原文表述及对应页码（页码以1984年普林斯顿大学出版社版本为准）：

    一、核心概念界定：合作、纷争与国际机制¶

    1. 国际合作的定义：基于“政策协调”的互动¶

    基欧汉明确区分“和谐（Harmony）”与“合作（Cooperation）”，指出合作的本质是通过政策调整实现利益互补，而非无冲突的自然状态：

    "Cooperation takes place only in situations in which actors perceive that their policies are actually or potentially in conflict, not where there is harmony. Cooperation should not be viewed as the absence of conflict, but rather as a reaction to conflict or potential conflict. Without the specter of conflict, there is no need to cooperate." (P53) "Cooperation involves mutual adjustment of policies: actors change their behavior to accommodate the preferences of others, in return for changes in others' behavior that accommodate their own preferences." (P51)

    2. 国际机制的定义：规则、规范与预期的集合¶

    基欧汉沿用克拉斯纳（Krasner）对国际机制的经典定义，强调其对降低交易成本、减少不确定性的功能：

    "International regimes are sets of implicit or explicit principles, norms, rules and decision-making procedures around which actors' expectations converge in a given area of international relations. Principles are beliefs of fact, causation, and rectitude. Norms are standards of behavior defined in terms of rights and obligations. Rules are specific prescriptions or proscriptions for action. Decision-making procedures are prevailing practices for making and implementing collective choice." (P57, 引用Krasner 1983年研究) "Regimes reduce transaction costs of legitimate bargains and increase them for illegitimate ones. They provide information to members, thereby reducing risks of making agreements. By linking issues, regimes create situations that are more like iterated Prisoners' Dilemma, in which cooperation may be rational, than like single-play Prisoners' Dilemma, in which it is not." (P90-91)

    二、核心理论：霸权稳定论的批判与制度功能主义¶

    1. 对“霸权稳定论”的反思：霸权是“有利条件”而非“必要条件”¶

    基欧汉承认霸权对机制创建的推动作用，但否定其为合作的“必要/充分条件”，指出霸权衰落不必然导致合作崩溃：

    "The theory of hegemonic stability holds that order in the world political economy depends on the preponderance of a single state. But this theory is flawed: hegemony can facilitate cooperation, but it is neither a necessary nor a sufficient condition for it. Even after the decline of hegemony, cooperation can persist if international regimes—created in the hegemonic era—continue to reduce uncertainty and transaction costs." (P32-33) "The United States was able to construct international regimes after World War II not only because of its material power, but also because it could provide collective goods (stable money, open markets) and enforce rules. Yet as U.S. power eroded, regimes such as GATT and the IMF did not collapse—they evolved, because states still benefited from the information and coordination they provided." (P185-186)

    2. 制度功能主义：国际机制的核心价值——降低“政治市场失灵”¶

    基欧汉借鉴“市场失灵”理论，指出国际机制通过解决信息不对称、道德风险、集体行动困境，使理性利己国家实现合作：

    "International regimes help to overcome 'political market failure': asymmetrical information (actors know more about their own intentions than others), moral hazard (agreements may alter incentives to cheat), and the free-rider problem (actors benefit from collective goods without contributing). By providing shared information and monitoring compliance, regimes make mutually beneficial agreements possible." (P82-83) "In a world of uncertainty and anarchy, regimes reduce the costs of bargaining by providing 'rules of thumb' for behavior. They make it easier for states to detect cheating (through monitoring) and to retaliate in a coordinated way—thus increasing the costs of defection." (P94-95)

    三、实证分析：霸权衰落与机制演变（货币、贸易、石油领域）¶

    1. 国际货币机制：从布雷顿森林体系到浮动汇率下的协调¶

    基欧汉指出，布雷顿森林体系崩溃后，货币合作未消失，而是通过“嵌入式自由主义（Embedded Liberalism）”原则延续：

    "The Bretton Woods monetary regime collapsed in 1971-73, but the principles of 'embedded liberalism' persisted: states accepted international economic openness, but retained the right to manage domestic economies for welfare goals. The IMF continued to play a role in balance-of-payments financing and macroeconomic surveillance—because states still needed a forum to coordinate policies and share information." (P186-187) "Even with floating exchange rates, central banks and finance ministries cooperate through informal networks and IMF-led surveillance. The 'non-system' of the 1980s was not chaos, but a regime with less explicit rules—still facilitating cooperation on debt rescheduling and currency stabilization." (P207-208)

    2. 贸易机制：GATT的韧性——从关税减让到“管理贸易”¶

    面对保护主义压力，GATT通过“议题联动”和“灵活规则”维持合作，体现机制的适应性：

    "The GATT regime faced pressures for protectionism in the 1970s-80s, yet it did not collapse. Instead, it evolved: 'voluntary export restraints' (VERs) and sector-specific agreements (e.g., the Multi-Fiber Arrangement) became common, even as tariff levels remained low. These were not 'liberal' outcomes, but they reflected cooperation to manage conflict—avoiding a return to 1930s-style trade wars." (P188-189) "GATT's value lies in its ability to link issues (e.g., agriculture to manufacturing) and to provide a framework for bargaining. Even when rules were bent, the regime's principles (reciprocity, nondiscrimination) provided a common language for negotiation—reducing the costs of reaching agreements." (P211-212)

    3. 石油机制：从公司主导到产消双方的博弈与合作¶

    基欧汉以国际能源机构（IEA）为例，说明后霸权时代“保险型机制”的作用：

    "The 1973 oil crisis revealed the failure of the old oil regime (dominated by Western companies). In response, the IEA was created as a consumers' regime: its core function was not to control prices, but to provide an emergency oil-sharing system and to coordinate demand restraint. Even though the IEA rarely activated its formal rules, it reduced uncertainty by facilitating information exchange and informal coordination—helping to avoid panic during the 1980 Iran-Iraq war." (P221-222) "OPEC, too, was a regime—albeit a weak one. It failed to act as a perfect cartel, but it provided a forum for producers to share information and negotiate production quotas. The post-1973 oil order was not hegemonic, but it was not chaotic: it was a system of 'bargained cooperation' between producers and consumers, mediated by institutions." (P234-235)

    四、核心结论：后霸权时代的合作逻辑——机制的“路径依赖”与适应性¶

    基欧汉强调，霸权创建的机制具有“路径依赖”，其价值不随霸权衰落而消失，反而成为无霸权合作的基础：

    "International regimes created in the hegemonic era are not 'hostages to power'; they have their own 'inertia' because they reduce transaction costs and shape expectations. Even as U.S. power declined, states continued to use regimes like GATT and the IMF because the alternative—ad hoc bargaining without shared rules—would be more costly." (P195-196) "Cooperation after hegemony is not inevitable, but it is possible. It depends on the ability of regimes to adapt to new power realities: to be more inclusive, to use flexible rules, and to focus on providing information rather than enforcing hierarchy. The future of world political economy lies not in a new hegemon, but in the resilience of these institutional arrangements." (P244-245)
