    Buzan B, Wæver O, de Wilde J.Security: A New Framework for Analysis[M].Boulder, CO: Lynne Rienner Publishers, 1998.¶

    Buzan B, Wæver O, de Wilde J.Security：A New Framework for Analysis[M].Boulder, CO：Lynne Rienner Publishers, 1998.

    Barry Buzan （巴里·布赞）是当代国际关系理论中极具影响力的学者之一，英国学派与安全研究领域的代表人物，尤其以安全化理论（securitization theory）和区域安全复合体理论（Regional Security Complex Theory, RSCT）闻名。

    从学术背景看，Buzan 长期任教于英国伦敦政治经济学院（LSE），后在哥本哈根大学与“哥本哈根学派”学者（如 Ole Wæver）形成紧密合作关系。他的研究横跨国际关系理论、安全研究、国际秩序与区域研究，在现实主义、制度主义与建构主义之间搭建起重要的分析桥梁。

    在理论贡献上，Buzan 的核心突破在于拓展了“安全”的分析边界。他反对将安全狭义理解为军事安全，系统提出安全可以被分析为军事、政治、经济、社会和环境五个部门，并强调安全并非客观给定，而是通过政治话语和社会互动被“建构”出来的。这一思想在其代表作Security: A New Framework for Analysis（1998）中得到系统阐述，成为安全研究领域的经典文献。

    Buzan 的另一项重要贡献是对国际秩序与世界社会结构的研究。他在From International to World Society（2004）等著作中，继承并发展了英国学派关于“国际社会”的分析传统，强调规则、制度、规范与权力结构在维持国际秩序中的共同作用。这使他的理论既不同于强调物质权力的结构现实主义，也不同于单纯聚焦制度合作的新自由制度主义。

    从理论定位看，Buzan 并非严格意义上的建构主义者。他更准确的学术身份是：

    英国学派学者 + 安全研究中的建构取向代表人物。

    他承认物质力量与结构约束的重要性，同时强调观念、话语和规范在安全与秩序形成中的关键作用，属于一种 “兼容结构与观念的中层理论取向”。

    《Security: A New Framework for Analysis》主要观点原文表述与页码 ¶

    一、核心理论框架相关 ¶

    （一）安全化理论（Securitization） ¶

    1. 安全化的定义

    2. 原文表述：“‘Security’ is the move that takes politics beyond the established rules of the game and frames the issue either as a special kind of politics or as above politics. Securitization can thus be seen as a more extreme version of politicization. In theory, any public issue can be located on the spectrum ranging from nonpoliticized (meaning the state does not deal with it and it is not in any other way made an issue of public debate and decision) through politicized (meaning the issue is part of public policy, requiring government decision and resource allocations or, more rarely, some other form of communal governance) to securitized (meaning the issue is presented as an existential threat, requiring emergency measures and justifying actions outside the normal bounds of political procedure).”

    3. 页码：p.23 - 24

    4. 安全化的成功条件

    5. 原文表述：“A successful securitization thus has three components (or steps): existential threats, emergency action, and effects on interunit relations by breaking free of rules. The distinguishing feature of securitization is a specific rhetorical structure (survival, priority of action ‘because if the problem is not handled now it will be too late, and we will not exist to remedy our failure’).”

    6. 页码：p.26

    7. 安全化与主体间性

    8. 原文表述：“Our argument is that securitization, like politicization, has to be understood as an essentially intersubjective process. Even if one wanted to take a more objectivist approach, it is unclear how this could be done except in cases in which the threat is unambiguous and immediate. (An example would be hostile tanks crossing the border; even here, ‘hostile’ is an attribute not of the vehicle but of the socially constituted relationship. A foreign tank could be part of a peacekeeping force.)”

    9. 页码：p.29 - 30

    （二）安全复合体理论（Security Complex Theory） ¶

    1. 传统安全复合体定义

    2. 原文表述：“A security complex is defined as a set of states whose major security perceptions and concerns are so interlinked that their national security problems cannot reasonably be analyzed or resolved apart from one another. The formative dynamics and structure of a security complex are generated by the states within that complex-by their security perceptions of, and interactions with, each other.”

    3. 页码：p.12

    4. 安全复合体的核心特征

    5. 原文表述：“Security interdependence is markedly more intense among the states inside such complexes than among states outside them. Security complexes are about the relative intensity of interstate security relations that lead to distinctive regional patterns shaped by both the distribution of power and historical relations of amity and enmity.”

    6. 页码：p.12

    7. 超越传统安全复合体理论的方向

    8. 原文表述：“One of the ways in which this book moves beyond classical security complex theory (CSCT) is by opening the analysis to a wider range of sectors. To what extent are regional patterns discernible when one no longer sticks to the state and privileges the political and military sectors? Will the security dynamics in the nontraditional sectors generate significant regional formations, or will their security logics place their main focus on higher (system) or lower (subunit) levels?”

    9. 页码：p.16

    二、五大安全领域相关 ¶

    （一）军事领域（Military Sector） ¶

    1. 军事安全的核心议程

    2. 原文表述：“When securitization is focused on external threats, military security is primarily about the two-level interplay between the actual armed offensive and defensive capabilities of states on the one hand and their perceptions of each other’s capabilities and intentions on the other. External threats range from fear of the complete obliteration of state, society, and people to gunboat diplomacy-style coercion and intimidation on particular issues of policy.”

    3. 页码：p.51

    4. 军事安全的区域化动态

    5. 原文表述：“In the military sector, the end of the Cold War has caused a marked shift away from global-level security concerns and toward regional and local-level ones. A case can be made that the international system is emerging from a long period in which the regional level of military security had been suppressed. Initially, this suppression took the form of European and, later, Japanese and U.S. imperialism.”

    6. 页码：p.61

    （二）环境领域（Environmental Sector） ¶

    1. 环境安全的双重议程

    2. 原文表述：“One of the most striking features of the environmental sector is the existence of two different agenda: a scientific agenda and a political agenda. Although they overlap and shape each other in part, the scientific agenda is typically embedded in the (mainly natural) sciences and nongovernmental activity. It is constructed outside the core of politics, mainly by scientists and research institutions, and offers a list of environmental problems that already or potentially hamper the evolution of present civilizations. The political agenda is essentially governmental and intergovernmental. It consists of the public decisionmaking process and public policies that address how to deal with environmental concerns.”

    3. 页码：p.72

    4. 环境安全的威胁逻辑

    5. 原文表述：“The basic logic of environmental security is that in a global perspective, humankind is living beyond the carrying capacity of the earth. In local and regional circumstances, this condition is often even more manifest. The exact meaning of the concept carrying capacity is disputed, but for the present context it can be defined as the total patterns of consumption the earth’s natural systems can support without undergoing degradation.”

    6. 页码：p.81

    （三）经济领域（Economic Sector） ¶

    1. 经济安全的争议性

    2. 原文表述：“The whole idea of economic security is exceedingly controversial and politicized. In a capitalist system, the very concept is fraught with contradictions and complications, not the least being that actors in a market are supposed to feel insecure; if they do not, the market will not produce its efficiencies.”

    3. 页码：p.95

    4. 经济安全的核心议题

    5. 原文表述：“Out of these general conditions grew a varied agenda of specific issues cast in terms of economic security: 1. The ability of states to maintain independent capability for military production in a global market... 2. The possibility that economic dependencies within the global market (particularly oil) will be exploited for political ends... 3. Fears that the global market would generate more losers than winners and would heighten existing inequalities... 4. Fears of (a) the dark side of capitalism... 5. Fears that the international economy itself would fall into crisis...”

    6. 页码：p.97 - 98

    （四）社会领域（Societal Sector） ¶

    1. 社会安全的定义

    2. 原文表述：“Societal insecurity exists when communities of whatever kind define a development or potentiality as a threat to their survival as a community. Despite the impression one might get from the present and, especially, previous presentations, the definition is not in terms of nations. Definitionally, societal security is about large, self-sustaining identity groups; what these are empirically varies in both time and place.”

    3. 页码：p.120

    4. 社会安全的主要威胁

    5. 原文表述：“The most common issues that have been viewed as threats to societal security are outlined here: 1. Migration-X people are being overrun or diluted by influxes of Y people... 2. Horizontal competition-although it is still X people living here, they will change their ways because of the overriding cultural and linguistic influence from neighboring culture Y... 3. Vertical competition-X people will stop seeing themselves as X, because there is either an integrating project or a secessionist-‘regionalist’ project...”

    6. 页码：p.120 - 121

    （五）政治领域（Political Sector） ¶

    1. 政治安全的核心

    2. 原文表述：“Political security is about the organizational stability of social order(s). The heart of the political sector is made up of threats to state sovereignty. Since threats can also be leveled through military means and the military sector has its own chapter, the political sector will take care of nonmilitary threats to sovereignty.”

    3. 页码：p.141

    4. 政治安全的威胁类型

    5. 原文表述：“Political threats are thus made to (1) the internal legitimacy of the political unit, which relates primarily to ideologies and other constitutive ideas and issues defining the state; and (2) the external recognition of the state, its external legitimacy. Threats from outside are not necessarily directed at sovereignty but can very well aim at its ideological legitimacy-that is, its domestic pillar.”

    6. 页码：p.144 - 145

    三、跨领域整合相关 ¶

    （一）领域间关联（Linkages Across Sectors） ¶

    • 原文表述：“It is impossible, however, to conclude on the basis of this study whether the regional security complexes are always identical in each sector. In principle, we could find that security dynamics in most sectors were regional but that the regions were different. Is military Europe the same as political Europe and societal Europe? Is economic East Asia the same as political East Asia and environmental East Asia? Whether these sectoral subsystems overlap and thus form coherent regions can be answered only tentatively and on a descriptive basis.”
    • 页码：p.166

    （二）行为体视角下的领域整合 ¶

    • 原文表述：“Sectors are not ontologically separate realms; they are not, like levels, separate subsystems. Some units, particularly the state, appear in several or all of the sectors, although at different strengths. We see sectors as a purely analytical device, as different lenses through which to see different views of the same issues. But although they are analytical devices, sectors exist not only in a theoretician’s head but also in policy heads, where the concept of security itself is the integrating force. Actors think about economics, politics, and other areas but judge their main security problems across the board.”
    • 页码：p.167 - 168
