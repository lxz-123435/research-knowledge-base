    Solow R M. Technical change and the aggregate production function[J].Review of Economics and Statistics, 1957, 39(3): 312–320.

    技术进步首次被实证证明是长期经济增长的主要来源，而非资本积累本身。“技术进步”被确立为长期增长的决定性变量，成为后续内生增长理论、创新经济学和产业政策分析的逻辑起点。

    Solow, R. M., 1957. “Technical change and the aggregate production function.” The Review of Economics and Statistics, 39(3)- 312–320..pdf

    Robert M. Solow（罗伯特·M·索洛）是20世纪最具影响力的宏观经济学家之一，美国新古典增长理论的奠基人，1987年诺贝尔经济学奖得主。他长期任教于麻省理工学院（MIT），其研究重点集中于经济增长、技术进步与长期发展机制。Solow最重要的学术贡献在于，将技术进步系统性地引入宏观经济分析框架，奠定了现代增长理论的基础，使“长期经济增长的来源”成为可分析、可测度的科学问题。

    Solow（1957）《Technical Change and the Aggregate Production Function》主要观点原文及页码

    一、核心方法与理论基础 ¶

    1. 区分技术变革与资本深化对人均产出的影响 ¶

    • 原文：“The new wrinkle I want to describe is an elementary way of segregating variations in output per head due to technical change from those due to changes in the availability of capital per head. Naturally, every additional bit of information has its price. In this case the price consists of one new required time series, the share of labor or property in total income, and one new assumption, that factors are paid their marginal products.”
    • 页码：312

    2. 总量生产函数与中性技术变革定义 ¶

    • 原文：“If Q represents output and K and L represent capital and labor inputs in 'physical' units, then the aggregate production function can be written as: $Q=F(K, L ; t)$. The variable t for time appears in F to allow for technical change... Shifts in the production function are defined as neutral if they leave marginal rates of substitution untouched but simply increase or decrease the output attainable from given inputs. In that case the production function takes the special form $Q=A(t) f(K, L)$ and the multiplicative factor $A(t)$ measures the cumulated effect of shifts over time.”
    • 页码：312 - 313

    3. 关键推导公式（人均产出增长分解） ¶

    • 原文：“Let $Q / L=q$, $K / L=k$, $w_{L}=1 - w_{K}$; note that $\dot{q}/q=\dot{Q}/Q - \dot{L}/L$ etc., and (2) becomes $\dot{q}/q=\dot{A}/A + w_{K}\dot{k}/k$ (2a). Now all we need to disentangle the technical change index $A(t)$ are series for output per man hour, capital per man hour, and the share of capital.”
    • 页码：313

    二、美国1909 - 1949年实证结果 ¶

    1. 技术变革的中性特征 ¶

    • 原文：“A scatter of $\triangle F/F$ against $K/L$ (not shown) indicates no trace of a relationship. So I may state it as a formal conclusion that over the period 1909 - 49, shifts in the aggregate production function netted out to be approximately neutral. Perhaps I should recall that I have defined neutrality to mean that the shifts were pure scale changes, leaving marginal rates of substitution unchanged at given capital/labor ratios.”
    • 页码：316

    2. 技术变革速度的阶段性差异 ¶

    • 原文：“There is some evidence that the average rate of progress in the years 1909 - 29 was smaller than that from 1930 - 49. The first 21 relative shifts average about 9/10 of one per cent per year, while the last 19 average 2 1/4 per cent per year. Even if the year 1929, which showed a strong downward shift, is moved from the first group to the second, there is still a contrast between an average rate of 1.2 per cent in the first half and 1.9 per cent in the second.”
    • 页码：316

    3. 人均产出增长的来源分解 ¶

    • 原文：“over the 40 year period output per man hour approximately doubled... It is possible to argue that about one - eighth of the total increase is traceable to increased capital per man hour, and the remaining seven - eighths to technical change... real GNP per man hour increased from \$.623 to \$1.275. Divide the latter figure by 1.809, which is the 1949 value for $A(t)$ and therefore the full shift factor for the 40 years. The result is a 'corrected' GNP per man hour, net of technical change, of \$.705. Thus about 8 cents of the 65 cent increase can be imputed to increased capital intensity, and the remainder to increased productivity.”
    • 页码：316 - 317

    4. 总量生产函数的特征（修正技术变革后） ¶

    • 原文：“Chart 4 gives an inescapable impression of curvature, of persistent but not violent diminishing returns. As for the possibility of approaching capital - saturation, there is no trace on this gross product level... Of these, (4d) is the Cobb - Douglas case... The correlation coefficients are uniformly so high that one hesitates to say any more than that all five functions, even the linear one, are about equally good at representing the general shape of the observed points. From the correlations alone, for what they are worth, it appears that the Cobb - Douglas function (4d) and the semilogarithmic (4b) are a bit better than the others.”
    • 页码：318 - 319

    三、结论与总结 ¶

    1. 核心结论汇总 ¶

    • 原文：“Among the conclusions which emerge from a crude application to American data, 1909 - 49, are the following:
    • 1. Technical change during that period was neutral on average.
    • 2. The upward shift in the production function was, apart from fluctuations, at a rate of about one per cent per year for the first half of the period and 2 per cent per year for the last half.
    • 3. Gross output per man hour doubled over the interval, with 87 1/2 per cent of the increase attributable to technical change and the remaining 12 1/2 per cent to increased use of capital.
    • 4. The aggregate production function, corrected for technical change, gives a distinct impression of diminishing returns, but the curvature is not violent.”

    将这一方法粗略应用于 1909—1949 年的美国相关数据后，可得出以下结论：

    1. 该时期内的技术变革整体呈现中性特征。
    2. 除短期波动因素外，生产函数的向上移动速率在该时期前半段约为年均 1%，后半段则提升至年均 2%。
    3. 在此期间，人均工时总产出实现翻倍增长，其中 87.5% 的增长可归因于技术变革，剩余 12.5% 则源于资本投入规模的扩大。

    4. 经技术变革因素修正后的总量生产函数，明确呈现出报酬递减趋势，但函数曲线的曲率变化相对平缓。

    5. neutral technical change：译为中性技术变革，是新古典增长理论的标准表述，指技术进步不会改变资本与劳动的边际产出比。

    6. production function：译为生产函数，为经济学核心概念，贯穿全文保持译法一致。
    7. gross output per man hour：译为人均工时总产出，精准对应 “单位工时的总产出量”，区别于 “人均产出” 的宽泛表述。
    8. diminishing returns：译为报酬递减趋势，是生产理论的经典概念；curvature译为曲率，保留计量经济学的术语属性。
    9. 页码：320
