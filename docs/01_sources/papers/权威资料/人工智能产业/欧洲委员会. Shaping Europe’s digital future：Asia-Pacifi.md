# 亚太地区

欧盟与亚太地区持续深化数字伙伴关系，推动研发、5G、人工智能等多个领域实现增长。

## 中国

欧盟委员会与中国保持年度信息通信技术（ICT）对话机制，涵盖信息通信技术、数字政策及监管相关议题。

该对话机制自 2009 年起由欧盟通信网络、内容与技术总局（DG Connect）与中国工业和信息化部联合举办，已促成双方在物联网领域的合作（相关成果见《欧中物联网联合白皮书》），并签署《5G 联合宣言》。此外，欧盟驻华代表团设有一名信息通信技术（ICT）顾问。

信息通信技术（ICT）与数字经济还与欧中各类贸易、科研相关对话及合作活动紧密关联，包括 “地平线 2020 计划”。

欧盟对外行动署与欧盟通信网络、内容与技术总局（DG Connect）共同主持欧中网络工作组（成立于 2012 年），旨在加强双方在网络领域的交流与合作。

2023 年 9 月 18 日，欧盟委员会在北京与中国举行第二届高级别数字对话会。会议涵盖平台与数据监管、人工智能、研发创新、工业数据跨境流动及网络销售产品安全等关键议题。

# Asia-Pacific

Asia-Pacific and the EU continue to strengthen their digital partnership ensuring growth in many areas such as research and development, 5G and AI.

## China

The European Commission maintains an annual ICT Dialogue with China covering ICT and digital policies as well as regulatory issues.

The ICT Dialogue has been jointly run by the Directorate-General for Communications Networks, Content and Technology (DG Connect) and the Chinese Ministry of Industry and Information Technology since 2009. This dialogue [has led to cooperation on the Internet of Things](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/library/eu-china-joint-white-paper-internet-things "has led to cooperation on the Internet of Things")and a[Joint Declaration on 5G](http://europa.eu/rapid/press-release_IP-15-5715_en.htm "Joint Declaration on 5G"). Furthermore, an ICT Counsellor is based at the EU Delegation in Beijing.

ICT and the digital economy are also closely linked with various EU-China trade and research related dialogues and cooperation activities, including[Horizon 2020](https://ec.europa.eu/programmes/horizon2020/en/horizon-2020-whats-it-china "Horizon 2020").

The European External Action Service and DG Connect co-chair an EU-China cyber task force founded in 2012 with the objective of enhancing exchanges and cooperation on cyber issues.

On the 18 September 2023, the Commission held its second [High-level Digital Dialogue](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/news/eu-china-commission-and-china-hold-second-high-level-digital-dialogue "High-level Digital Dialogue") with China in Beijing. The event covered issues such as s platforms and data regulation, Artificial Intelligence, research and innovation, cross-border flow of industrial data, and the safety of products sold online.
