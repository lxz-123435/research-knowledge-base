## 一、跨境监管摩擦与合规冲突的原文依据 &#x20;

### 1. 监管规则碎片化与域外适用冲突 &#x20;

- **页码**：Section 3.1（P28-29） &#x20;
- **中文表述**：人工智能的跨境开发、部署与使用往往涉及多个司法管辖区，亟需国际协调。人工智能模型的训练数据可能来自多个国家，且通过国际云服务获取；人工智能开发的不同阶段发生在具有不同法律框架的多个司法管辖区，这使得单个国家难以对人工智能全生命周期进行监管。人工智能实物供应链涉及来自全球南北国家的材料、硬件和劳动力。若缺乏协调的全球方法，分散的国家政策可能导致监管套利、不一致性和潜在漏洞，进而造成人工智能治理的缺口甚至“逐底竞争”。 &#x20;
- **英文原文**：AI development, deployment, and use are often cross-jurisdictional, necessitating international coordination. AI models may be trained on datasets collected from numerous countries and accessed through international cloud services. Different stages of AI development occur in multiple jurisdictions with varying legal frameworks, making it challenging for individual countries to regulate the entire AI lifecycle. The material AI supply chain involves materials, hardware, and labor sourced from a wide array of countries across both the Global North and South. Without a coordinated global approach, disparate national policies can lead to regulatory arbitrage, inconsistencies, and potential loopholes, resulting in gaps or even a ‘race to the bottom’ in AI governance. &#x20;

### 2. 合规冲突与企业运营负担 &#x20;

- **页码**：Section 4（P31-32） &#x20;
- **中文表述**：各国监管框架的差异可能导致监管碎片化，不同司法管辖区制定的不兼容或冲突的法规会造成重叠要求，给在多个地区运营的企业带来合规负担。这种碎片化和重叠可能通过增加合规的复杂性和成本，阻碍本地人工智能创新生态系统的成长和规模化发展。 &#x20;
- **英文原文**：There is a significant risk of regulatory fragmentation, where different jurisdictions develop incompatible or conflicting regulations. This can lead to overlapping requirements that create compliance burdens for businesses operating in multiple regions. Such fragmentation and overlap can hinder the growth and scalability of local AI innovation ecosystems by increasing the complexity and cost of compliance. &#x20;

## 二、监管套利与“逐底竞争”风险的原文依据 &#x20;

### 1. 监管套利的制度诱因 &#x20;

- **页码**：Section 6.4（P82） &#x20;
- **中文表述**：人工智能治理需要国际协调，以防止“逐底竞争”——即私营企业寻求将其最具危害性的活动转移到监管壁垒较低的地区。确保监管公平竞争环境对较小型、欠发达国家尤为重要，否则这些国家可能面临降低监管门槛以鼓励本地创新或吸引外国投资的压力。 &#x20;
- **英文原文**：International coordination on AI governance is needed in order to prevent a ‘race to the bottom’ – i.e., to prevent ‘regulatory arbitrage’ as private firms seek to relocate their most harmful activities to areas of low regulatory barriers. Ensuring a level regulatory playing field is particularly important for smaller, less developed states who may otherwise face pressures to lower guardrails in order to encourage local innovation or foster foreign investment. &#x20;

### 2. “逐底竞争”对治理效能的削弱 &#x20;

- **页码**：Section 7.2（P89） &#x20;
- **中文表述**：人工智能治理若缺乏协调，可能导致各国为吸引企业投资而竞相降低监管标准，引发“逐底竞争”，进而削弱全球人工智能治理水平。例如，部分国家可能放松人工智能算法透明度要求、降低数据隐私保护标准，以降低企业合规成本，这不仅会加剧算法偏见、数据泄露等风险，还会扩大“南北鸿沟”，破坏人工智能治理的公平性。 &#x20;
- **英文原文**：Without coordination in AI governance, countries may compete to lower regulatory standards to attract business investment, triggering a ‘race to the bottom’ that undermines global AI governance standards. For instance, some countries may relax requirements for AI algorithm transparency and lower data privacy protection standards to reduce corporate compliance costs. This not only exacerbates risks such as algorithmic bias and data breaches but also widens the ‘North-South divide’ and undermines the fairness of AI governance. &#x20;

## 三、制度差异削弱治理效能与公平性的原文依据 &#x20;

### 1. 治理效能受损：规则碎片化与协作缺失 &#x20;

- **页码**：Section 3.1（P28） &#x20;
- **中文表述**：人工智能治理的核心挑战之一是“步调问题”（pacing problem），即监管和治理框架难以跟上人工智能技术的快速发展。制定新法律和政策可能需要数月甚至数年时间，而在此期间人工智能技术持续进步，导致治理缺口。此外，人工智能供应链的复杂性和透明度缺失，使得难以追溯潜在问题的根源，也难以确定监管干预的实际切入点。 &#x20;
- **英文原文**：One of the primary issues is the rapid pace of AI development... This rapid evolution, often referred to as the ‘pacing problem,’ means that regulatory and governance frameworks struggle to keep up. Developing new laws and policies can take months or even years, during which AI technologies continue to advance, creating governance gaps... The complexity and lack of transparency in these supply chains make it difficult to trace the origins of potential issues and identify practical points for regulatory intervention. &#x20;

### 2. 治理公平性受损：南北鸿沟与参与失衡 &#x20;

- **页码**：Section 6.4（P82） &#x20;
- **中文表述**：若缺乏全球规则制定合作，主要位于北半球的大型科技公司（它们主导人工智能的开发和部署）可能只选择遵守其重要优先市场适用的监管框架。由于市场规模和/或地缘政治影响力的不平衡，较小型的新兴市场和发展中经济体可能难以对位于其他司法管辖区的人工智能开发者施加监管影响。若以参与性和稳健的方式建立人工智能治理国际协调机制，新兴市场和发展中经济体将有机会确保其需求和关切在人工智能治理中得到体现。 &#x20;
- **英文原文**：In the absence of global cooperation on rulemaking, large technology companies leading AI development and deployment based overwhelmingly in the Global North may choose only to comply with the regulatory frameworks applicable in their large priority markets. Smaller EMDEs may therefore find it difficult to exert regulatory influence over AI developers based in other jurisdictions due to imbalances in market size and/or relative geopolitical influence. International coordination on AI governance, if created in a participatory and robust manner, gives EMDEs the opportunity to ensure their needs and concerns are reflected in how AI is governed.
