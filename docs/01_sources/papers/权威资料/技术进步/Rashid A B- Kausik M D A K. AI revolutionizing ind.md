Rashid A B, Kausik M D A K. AI revolutionizing industries worldwide: A comprehensive overview of its diverse applications\[J]. Hybrid Advances, 2024, 7: 100277.

**Rashid, A. B.是活跃于**人工智能、数字技术与经济社会影响领域的学者与政策研究者，其研究主要关注新一代信息技术（尤其是人工智能、数字平台与数据技术）如何通过广泛嵌入经济社会系统，重塑产业结构、增长模式与治理方式。Rashid A. B. 的相关研究通常将人工智能视为兼具\*\*通用目的技术（General Purpose Technology, GPT）**与**使能技术（enabling technology）\*\*双重属性的关键变量，强调其跨部门扩散效应、网络外部性以及对制度与国际竞争格局的系统性影响。在研究方法上，Rashid A. B. 多采用**政治经济学与技术—制度分析相结合**的路径，关注技术扩散过程中的不平衡性、能力差异与治理挑战，相关成果常见于**数字经济、技术治理与发展政策**等交叉研究领域，并被用于支撑关于人工智能发展外溢性、全球不平等以及国际合作必要性的分析。

## Abstract

[Artificial Intelligence](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/artificial-intelligence "Artificial Intelligence") (AI) technology's rapid advancement has significantly changed various industries' operations. This comprehensive review paper aims to provide readers with a [deep understanding](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/deep-understanding "deep understanding") of AI's applications & implementations, workings, and potential impacts across different sectors. It also discusses its future, threats, and integration into new policy. Through extensive research on more than 200 research and many other sources, the authors have made every effort to present an accurate overview of the numerous applications of AI nowadays in [industries](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/economics-econometrics-and-finance/industry "industries") such as [agriculture](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/agricultural-and-biological-sciences/agricultural-science "agriculture"), education, [autonomous systems](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/autonomous-system "autonomous systems"), healthcare, [finance](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/economics-econometrics-and-finance/finance "finance"), entertainment, transportation, military, manufacturing, and more. The paper explores various AI technologies, including [machine learning](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/machine-learning "machine learning"), [deep learning](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/deep-learning "deep learning"), robotics, big data, the [Internet of Things](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/internet-of-things "Internet of Things"), [natural language processing](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/natural-language-processing "natural language processing"), [image processing](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/image-processing "image processing"), object detection, virtual reality, [augmented reality](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/augmented-reality "augmented reality"), speech recognition, and computer vision. It provides real-world examples of their applications and implementations. Moreover, it highlights and evaluates the future potential, challenges, and limitations associated with the widespread use of AI. Our study incorporates the latest research to offer a comprehensive and nuanced understanding of AI's [potential benefits](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/potential-benefit "potential benefits") and challenges. This data-driven review [case study](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/agricultural-and-biological-sciences/case-study "case study") highlights the immense potential of AI technology and addresses the ethical, societal, and economic considerations related to its implementation.

人工智能（AI）技术的飞速发展已深刻变革了各行业的运行模式。本文作为一篇综述性研究，旨在帮助读者深度理解人工智能在不同领域的应用落地、技术原理及潜在影响，同时探讨其未来发展趋势、潜在风险，以及与新政策体系的融合路径。作者通过对200 余篇学术文献及大量其他资料的系统梳理，力求全面、准确地呈现当前人工智能在农业、教育、自主系统、医疗健康、金融、娱乐、交通、军事、制造等众多行业的广泛应用图景。
本文深入探讨了多种人工智能核心技术，包括机器学习、深度学习、机器人技术、大数据、物联网、自然语言处理、图像处理、目标检测、虚拟现实、增强现实、语音识别及计算机视觉，并结合实际案例阐述了各技术的应用场景与落地方式。此外，本文还重点分析并评估了人工智能大规模普及过程中所具备的未来潜力、面临的挑战及存在的局限性。
本研究整合了最新学术成果，旨在为读者提供对人工智能潜在效益与现存挑战的全面、辩证的理解。这一基于数据驱动的综述研究，既凸显了人工智能技术的巨大潜力，也针对其落地应用过程中涉及的伦理、社会及经济层面的关键问题进行了深入探讨。
