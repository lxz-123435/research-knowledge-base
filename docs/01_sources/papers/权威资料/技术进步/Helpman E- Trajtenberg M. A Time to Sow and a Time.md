Helpman E, Trajtenberg M. A Time to Sow and a Time to Reap：Growth Based on General Purpose Technologies\[R]. Cambridge, MA：National Bureau of Economic Research, 1998. (NBER Working Paper No. 4854).

**Elhanan Helpman（埃尔哈南·赫尔普曼）** 是当代最具影响力的国际经济学家和增长理论学者之一，在**通用目的技术（GPT）理论、内生经济增长、国际贸易与技术扩散**等领域作出了奠基性贡献。

在经济学界，Helpman 通常被视为**继 Romer、Aghion 之后，将技术进步与产业结构、国际贸易深度融合的关键人物**

# Helpman & Trajtenberg（1994）《A Time to Sow and a Time to Reap：Growth Based on General Purpose Technologies》主要观点原文表述及页码 &#x20;

## 一、通用技术（GPTs）的核心特征 &#x20;

1. **普遍性（Pervasiveness）**：“GPTs are extremely pervasive; that is, they are used as inputs by a wide range of sectors in the economy. This stems from the fact that GPTs perform some generic function (such as continuous rotary motion for the steam engine, or binary logic for micro-electronics) that happens to have virtually universal applicability throughout the economy.”（p.1） &#x20;
2. **持续技术进步潜力**：“The potential for continuous technical advances in the GPT itself, which manifests itself ex post as sustained improvements in performance.”（p.1） &#x20;
3. **与用户部门的互补性**：“The presence of complementarities with the user sectors, which can arise in manufacturing or in the R\&D technology.”（p.1） &#x20;

## 二、GPT驱动的增长周期特征 &#x20;

1. **周期两阶段划分**：“During the first phase of each cycle output and productivity grow slowly or even decline, and it is only in the second phase that growth starts in earnest.”（p.1，摘要部分） &#x20;
2. **第一阶段（播种期）特征**：“During the first phase output and productivity experience negative growth, the real wage rate stagnates, and the share of profits in GDP declines. The benefits from a more advanced GPT manifest themselves during the second phase, after enough complementary inputs have been developed for it.”（p.2） &#x20;
3. **第二阶段（收获期）特征**：“During this later phase there is a spell of growth, with rising output, real wages and profits. Over the entire cycle the economy grows at the rate determined by the rate of advance in the GPT itself.”（p.2） &#x20;
4. **新技术引入的即时影响**：“First, the immediate impact of the arrival of the new, more productive GPT, is to lower output.”（p.2） &#x20;

## 三、模型核心设定与推导 &#x20;

1. **最终品生产函数**：“Suppose that a final good is produced with the aid of a General Purpose Technology (GPT) i and an assembly of a continuum of components $x_{i}(j)$ $j \in[0, n_{i}]$, that have to be compatible with the particular GPT in use, where $n_{i}$ denotes the number (measure) of available components. The production function is given by $Q_{i}=\lambda^{i} D_{i}, \lambda>1$, where $D_{i}=\left[\int_{0}^{n_{i}} x_{i}(j)^{\alpha} d j\right]^{1 / \alpha}, 0<\alpha<1$.”（p.3） &#x20;
2. **组件定价规则**：“Under these circumstances each component manufacturer equates marginal revenue to marginal costs, and hence all components will be equally priced according to $p_{i}(j)=p=\frac{1}{\alpha} w$.”（p.4，公式3） &#x20;
3. **GPT切换条件**：“In general, there will be a switch from the $i^{th}$ GPT to the $(i+1)^{th}$ as soon as, $n_{i+1}>\eta n_{i}, 0<\eta \equiv 1 / \lambda^{\alpha /(1-\alpha)}<1$.”（p.5，公式6） &#x20;
4. **研发均衡条件**：“free entry into R\&D implies the equilibrium condition $v_{i} \leq w a$, with equality whenever $\dot{n}_{i}>0$.”（p.6，公式10） &#x20;
5. **劳动资源约束**：“an economy with m general purpose technologies and L units of labor faces the resource constraint $a \sum_{i=1}^{m} \dot{n}_{i}+\sum_{i=1}^{m} b_{i} Q_{i}=L$.”（p.6，公式11） &#x20;

## 四、关键经济变量动态 &#x20;

1. **实际GDP表达式**：“To calculate real GDP we divide nominal GDP by the price of final output $p_{Q}$ as defined in (5), yielding the following measure: $G(t)=\left[L+\frac{1-\alpha}{w(t)}\right] \alpha \lambda^{k} n_{k}(t)^{(1-\alpha) / \alpha}$, where k is the index of the general purpose technology actually used in the production of final output.”（p.15，公式23） &#x20;
2. **实际GDP的周期波动**：“real GDP jumps downwards at the beginning of each new cycle... Beyond the fall in output right at the start of a cycle, it is apparent from (23) that real GDP keeps declining throughout the first phase... Real GDP increases throughout the second phase as the number of components for the prevailing GPT keeps rising, and as the wage rate declines.”（p.15-16） &#x20;
3. **实际工资与利润**：“real wages remain constant during phase 1 of each cycle, and then rise over the course of phase 2... Real profits, on the other hand, do drop at the beginning of each cycle... and decrease further along phase 1... During phase 2 though, real profits rise continuously.”（p.19） &#x20;
4. **要素份额**：“the labor share is $w L /(1-\alpha+w L)$, and the share of profits is $(1-\alpha) /(1-\alpha+w L)$... the profit share is pro-cyclical (as is the case with real profits), whereas the labor share is counter-cyclical.”（p.19） &#x20;

## 五、模型拓展：多部门扩散与技能溢价 &#x20;

1. **多部门GPT扩散机制**：“as the number of available components for a new GPT increases over time, a larger fraction of sectors switches to manufacture with it. In fact, given $\lambda(\cdot)$, we can derive a non-decreasing function $f(n_{i}/n_{i-1})$, that describes the fraction of sectors that manufacture with the newest general purpose technology i.”（p.24） &#x20;
2. **多部门GDP增长驱动因素**：“in the present case there is an additional force contributing to productivity growth, and that is the rate of diffusion of the latest GPT in the manufacturing sector; i.e., the rise in $Z_{i}$.”（p.25） &#x20;
3. **技能工资动态**：“the relative wage rate of skilled workers rises during the first phase. However, during phase 2 the wage rate of skilled workers is always lower than its peak at the end of phase 1. Therefore the relative wage rate of skilled workers has to decline at least at the beginning of phase 2...”（p.26） &#x20;
4. **不同技能劳动者实际工资**：“the real wage rate of unskilled workers, $w/p_{Q}$, is constant in phase 1 and rising in phase 2... the real wage rate of skilled workers, $w_{H}/p_{Q}$, rises during phase 1... In phase 2 the real wage rate of skilled workers may decline.”（p.27） &#x20;

## 六、模拟与实证关联 &#x20;

1. **模拟参数范围**：“the range of parameter values used in those simulations were: $\lambda \in[1.3,28]$, $\alpha \in[0.1,0.6]$, and $\rho \in[0.025,0.075]$.”（p.21） &#x20;
2. **α的双重影响**：“α has two opposite effects: on the one hand it reduces $g_{1}$ and $g_{2}$, but on the other hand it shortens a great deal the length of the first phase... A higher α implies a lower η... hence ending the first phase. The negative impact of α on both $g_{1}$ and $g_{2}$ is due to the fact that α represents in this context an aspect of appropriability.”（p.22-23） &#x20;
3. **与历史经验的契合**：“The growth path thus obtained in the model seems to correspond to the historical record of productivity growth following the introduction of electricity a century ago, and may likewise resemble the economy-wide impact of computerization of the last few decades... David's (1990) documentation of the introduction of electricity is of particular interest. As he has shown, substantial productivity gains appeared only about four decades after the introduction of dynamos.”（p.2, p.17） &#x20;

## 七、政策启示与结论 &#x20;

1. **政策的周期依赖性**：“the effectiveness (and even desirability) of different policy measures may well depend upon where along the cycle one stands... If one stands at the beginning of a new cycle... foster competition in the components sector. If one were standing at the beginning of the second phase... increasing appropriability, which implies exactly the opposite.”（p.29） &#x20;
2. **GPT增长的核心结论**：“the sequential and cumulative nature of such investments \[complementary inputs] may induce different phases along each cycle... there is a threshold level of complementary inputs that need to be developed before the latest GPT can outcompete and displace the previous one.”（p.30） &#x20;
3. **模型局限性与未来方向**：“we have ignored the endogenous character of advances in the GPT itself, and the associated (positive) feedback going from the user sectors to the GPT... its (hopefully transient) omission is due only to the modeling difficulties that we have encountered.”（p.31）
