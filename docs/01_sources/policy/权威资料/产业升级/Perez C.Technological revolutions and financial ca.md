Perez C.Technological revolutions and financial capital：The dynamics of bubbles and golden ages\[M]. Cheltenham：Edward Elgar Publishing, 2002.

**Carlota Perez（卡洛塔·佩雷斯）是当代极具影响力的经济学家与技术经济学、经济史学者，长期研究技术革命、通用目的技术与经济结构转型**问题。她曾任教并研究于伦敦政治经济学院（LSE）等学术机构，其研究以跨越经济学、经济史和制度分析见长。Perez 的代表性贡献在于提出并系统阐释了 **“技术革命—金融资本—生产范式转换”** 的分析框架，揭示重大技术突破如何在长周期内重塑产业结构、发展模式和国家竞争力。

《Technological Revolutions and Financial Capital：The Dynamics of Bubbles and Golden Ages》主要观点及原文摘录（含页码标注） &#x20;

## 一、核心框架：技术革命与金融资本的动态互动模型 &#x20;

### 1.1 技术革命的本质与“技术-经济范式” &#x20;

- **原文**：“A technological revolution can be defined as a powerful and highly visible cluster of new and dynamic technologies, products and industries, capable of bringing about an upheaval in the whole fabric of the economy and of propelling a long-term upsurge of development.”（Chapter 2，p.8） &#x20;
- **原文**：“A techno-economic paradigm is, then, a best-practice model made up of a set of all-pervasive generic technological and organizational principles, which represent the most effective way of applying a particular technological revolution and of using it for modernizing and rejuvenating the whole of the economy.”（Chapter 2，p.15） &#x20;
- **解读**：技术革命是一组能颠覆经济结构、推动长期增长的新技术与新产业集群；而“技术-经济范式”是伴随技术革命形成的“最佳实践模型”，涵盖通用技术与组织原则，指导全经济领域的现代化转型（如信息革命中的“去中心化网络”原则）。 &#x20;

### 1.2 发展浪潮的两阶段与四相位划分 &#x20;

- **原文**：“Each surge goes through two periods of a very different nature, each lasting about three decades... the first half can be termed the installation period... the second half is the deployment period... each period in turn goes through two different phases... the recurring sequence is made up of four phases lasting around a decade each: Irruption, Frenzy – turning point – Synergy and Maturity.”（Chapter 4，p.36；Chapter 5，p.47） &#x20;
- **原文**：“The installation period is one of tense coexistence of two paradigms... the deployment period, when the fabric of the whole economy is rewoven and reshaped by the modernizing power of the triumphant paradigm.”（Chapter 4，p.36-37） &#x20;
- **解读**：每轮技术革命驱动的“发展浪潮”分为两个核心阶段——“安装期”（新技术突破与旧范式冲突，约20-30年）与“部署期”（新技术范式全面扩散，约20-30年）；每个阶段又细分为两个相位，形成“爆发期（Irruption）-狂热期（Frenzy）-转折点（Turning Point）-协同期（Synergy）-成熟期（Maturity）”的完整循环。 &#x20;

## 二、技术革命的历史演进与特征 &#x20;

### 2.1 五次技术革命的历史脉络 &#x20;

- **原文**：“Five successive technological revolutions have shaped the last two centuries...&#x20;
- 1\. The ‘Industrial Revolution’ (Britain, 1771, Arkwright’s mill);&#x20;
- 2\. Age of Steam and Railways (Britain, 1829, ‘Rocket’ steam engine);&#x20;
- 3\. Age of Steel, Electricity and Heavy Engineering (USA/Germany, 1875, Carnegie’s Bessemer steel plant);&#x20;
- 4\. Age of Oil, Automobile and Mass Production (USA, 1908, Ford’s Model-T);&#x20;
- 5\. Age of Information and Telecommunications (USA, 1971, Intel microprocessor).”（Chapter 2，Table 2.1，p.11） &#x20;
- **解读**：作者梳理了18世纪以来五次技术革命的核心国家、“大爆炸事件”（Big-Bang）与标志性技术，强调每次革命均以“低成本关键投入”（如蒸汽机、电力、微芯片）为核心，带动基础设施与产业集群变革。 &#x20;

### 2.2 技术革命的生命周期与扩散规律 &#x20;

- **原文**：“Technological revolutions and paradigms have a life cycle of about half a century, which more or less follows the type of logistic curve characteristic of any innovation... Phase one: explosive growth of new industries; Phase two: fast diffusion of infrastructure; Phase three: full deployment of the paradigm; Phase four: maturity and approaching exhaustion.”（Chapter 3，p.29-30） &#x20;
- **原文**：“The propagation of each paradigm spreads in ripple-like fashion... both from sector to sector across the industrial structure and geographically inside each country and across the world.”（Chapter 6，p.63） &#x20;
- **解读**：技术革命的生命周期呈“S型曲线”，从爆发期的快速增长到成熟期的潜力耗尽；其扩散遵循“涟漪效应”——先在核心国家的核心产业落地，再逐步向边缘国家与传统产业渗透，存在显著的地域与行业时滞。 &#x20;

## 三、金融资本的行为演变与角色矛盾 &#x20;

### 3.1 金融资本与生产资本的功能分离 &#x20;

- **原文**：“Financial capital represents the criteria and behavior of those agents who possess wealth in the form of money or other paper assets... Production capital embodies the motives and behaviors of those agents who generate new wealth by producing goods or performing services... The functional distinction is essential to the nature of the capitalist system.”（Chapter 7，p.71-72） &#x20;
- **原文**：“Financial capital is mobile by nature while production capital is basically tied to concrete products... Financial capital can successfully invest without much knowledge of what it does; production capital depends on specific knowledge of product, process and markets.”（Chapter 7，p.72-73） &#x20;
- **解读**：金融资本以“钱生钱”为目标，追求流动性与短期收益；生产资本以“创造真实财富”为核心，依赖技术、市场知识与长期投入。二者的功能分离是资本主义的本质特征，既为技术革命提供资金，也可能因短期投机导致系统失衡。 &#x20;

### 3.2 金融资本在各相位的行为特征 &#x20;

#### 3.2.1 爆发期（Irruption）：金融资本与技术革命的“热恋” &#x20;

- **原文**：“In the irruption phase... financial capital begins a passionate relationship with the emerging production capital. The new revolutionary entrepreneurs soon outstrip the profit-making potential of established sectors, and there is a rush of financial capital towards them, readily developing new appropriate instruments.”（Chapter 9，p.90-91） &#x20;
- **案例**：“Venture capital in the microelectronics revolution (1970s) emerged to fund garage innovators; joint-stock companies in the railway revolution (1830s) concentrated capital and spread risks.”（Chapter 9，p.92） &#x20;

#### 3.2.2 狂热期（Frenzy）：金融资本的“赌场化”与脱钩 &#x20;

- **原文**：“Frenzy is the tumultuous period when financial capital takes off on its own... the paper economy decouples from the real economy, finance decouples from production... asset inflation sets in while debt mounts at a reckless rhythm; much of it to enter the casino.”（Chapter 10，p.99-102） &#x20;
- **原文**：“The NASDAQ bubble (1990s) saw price-earnings ratios augment out of all proportions... Yahoo’s stock price exceeded that of Eastman Kodak, a company with massive physical assets.”（Chapter 10，p.112-113；Figure 11.1，p.119） &#x20;

#### 3.2.3 协同期（Synergy）：金融资本回归服务生产 &#x20;

- **原文**：“In the synergy phase... real growth in production becomes the basic source of wealth... financial capital acts as the true support of production capital... it is the time when aggregate statistics are consistent for safe extrapolation.”（Chapter 12，p.127-128） &#x20;
- **案例**：“Post-WWII USA (1940s-1960s): corporate financing relied on internal funds (75%) rather than stock flotations; Wall Street’s influence faded as production capital led mass production expansion.”（Chapter 12，p.128-129） &#x20;

## 四、转折点（Turning Point）：危机、 regulation与范式重构 &#x20;

### 4.1 转折点的触发机制与结构矛盾 &#x20;

- **原文**：“The turning point is triggered by the collapse of the financial bubble... three structural tensions make the frenzy unsustainable: 1. gap between paper wealth and real wealth; 2. mismatch between potential supply and demand profile; 3. social polarization between rich and poor.”（Chapter 11，p.115-117） &#x20;
- **原文**：“The 1929 crash and 1930s Great Depression lasted so long because the institutional framework (Keynesian policies) was not established until WWII; resistance to regulation prolonged the crisis.”（Chapter 11，p.125-126） &#x20;

### 4.2 转折点的政策核心：金融监管与制度适配 &#x20;

- **原文**：“At the turning point, adequate regulation of financial capital must be established... to curb casino practices and reattach finance to production. This includes accountancy standards, separation of savings and investment banks, and global regulatory coordination.”（Chapter 11，p.120-121） &#x20;
- **原文**：“The Bretton Woods system (1944) established rules for international finance (USD as reserve currency, IMF/World Bank) – a key institutional innovation enabling the synergy phase of the mass-production paradigm.”（Chapter 12，p.129-130） &#x20;

## 五、技术革命与金融资本互动的核心结论 &#x20;

### 5.1 周期性规律的本质驱动因素 &#x20;

- **原文**：“Three features drive development by surges: 1. Technical change occurs by clusters of innovations (technological revolutions); 2. Financial and production capital are functionally separate with different profit criteria; 3. The socio-institutional framework has greater inertia than the techno-economic sphere.”（Chapter 14，p.155） &#x20;
- **解读**：技术革命的集群性、金融与生产资本的功能差异、制度变革的滞后性，三者共同导致资本主义经济呈现“繁荣-泡沫-危机-重构”的周期性循环，而非线性增长。 &#x20;

### 5.2 对政策与理论的启示 &#x20;

- **原文**：“Policy must respond to a moving target... at each phase, the range for viable responses is shaped by the paradigm’s nature. A ‘golden age’ is possible only if institutions align with the new technology – e.g., global regulation for the information revolution.”（Chapter 15，p.163-166） &#x20;
- **原文**：“Economic theories are context-dependent: Keynesians dominated the deployment phase of mass production; monetarists gained influence in the installation phase of the information revolution.”（Chapter 15，p.161-162） &#x20;
- **解读**：政策需适配技术范式的阶段特征（如信息革命需全球金融监管），且经济理论的有效性随技术-金融周期动态变化，不存在“永恒真理”。
