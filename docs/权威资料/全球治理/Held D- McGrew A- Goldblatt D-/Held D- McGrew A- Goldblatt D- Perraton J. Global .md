# Held D, McGrew A, Goldblatt D, Perraton J.*Global Transformations: Politics, Economics and Culture*\[M]. Cambridge: Polity Press, 1999.

Held D, McGrew A, Goldblatt D, Perraton J.*Global Transformations：Politics, Economics and Culture*\[M]. Cambridge：Polity Press, 1999.

**David Held（戴维·赫尔德，1951—2019）** 是当代最具影响力的**政治理论家和全球治理研究学者**之一，被公认为**全球治理（Global Governance）与民主理论**领域的奠基性人物。他长期任教于**伦敦政治经济学院（LSE）**，并曾担任**伦敦政治经济学院政治与国际关系学院院长**，其研究深刻塑造了学界对**全球化、国家主权与跨国治理结构**的理解。

### **一、学术定位：全球化时代的治理理论奠基者**

Held 的核心学术关切可以概括为一个根本问题：

> **在全球化不断深化、权力与问题跨越国界流动的背景下，政治治理应如何重构其制度形态与民主基础？**

他反对两种极端立场：

- 将全球化视为“国家终结”的简单判断
- 或坚持国家主权完全不变的传统现实主义视角

而是提出一种更具解释力的中间路径：**全球化重塑而非消解国家治理，催生多层级、网络化的治理结构**。

***

### **二、核心学术贡献一：全球治理与“去疆域化”政治**

Held 在其代表性著作*Global Transformations*（1999）中系统提出：

> **全球化的本质并非单向度的经济一体化，而是政治、经济与文化关系在空间上的“去疆域化（deterritorialization）”。**

这一判断意味着：

- 权力和风险不再被严格限制在民族国家边界之内
- 治理问题呈现出跨国性、公共性和系统性
- 单一国家难以通过封闭制度实现有效治理

这一理论为**环境治理、金融监管、数字治理和人工智能治理**等议题提供了统一的分析框架。

***

### **三、核心学术贡献二：多层级全球治理结构（Multi-level Governance）**

Held 提出，全球治理并非由某个“世界政府”实现，而是由：

- 国家政府
- 国际组织
- 区域机制
- 跨国企业与社会组织

共同构成的**多层级、网络化治理体系**。

在这一体系中：

- 国家仍是关键行动者
- 但其治理能力需要通过国际规则与制度合作加以延展

这一观点对理解**人工智能规则国际合作**尤为重要，因为 AI 治理恰恰需要在国家、区域和全球层面协同推进。

***

### **四、核心学术贡献三：全球民主与制度正当性**

除治理结构外，Held 还高度关注全球治理的**民主正当性问题**。他提出“**世界主义民主（Cosmopolitan Democracy）**”理念，主张：

> **跨国规则制定应在尊重国家主权的基础上，提升透明度、参与度和责任机制。**

这一思想为当代关于：

- 全球规则制定公平性
- 技术治理伦理
- 国际制度包容性

的讨论提供了重要理论资源。

### 一、《Global Transformations：Politics, Economics and Culture》核心观点原文表述及页码 &#x20;

以下内容基于文档《Global Transformations：Politics, Economics and Culture》（David Held等著，1999年版），结合“全球治理通过制度协调提升跨国公共事务治理有效性”的核心逻辑，梳理书中关键观点及对应页码： &#x20;

#### 1. 全球治理的制度协调基础：国际机制与多层治理架构 &#x20;

- **核心观点**：全球治理的有效性依赖规范化的国际机制（regimes）、多边组织及法律框架，这些制度工具通过明确权利义务、统一行动标准，减少跨国事务中的冲突与不确定性，形成“多层治理”（multilayered governance）架构。 &#x20;
- “**国际机制**可以定义为‘在国际关系特定议题领域中，行为体预期趋同所围绕形成的、隐性或显性的原则、规则与决策程序’（克拉斯纳，1983，第 2 页）。国际机制并非临时性或权宜性的协定；相反，它可被视为处于国际体系基本权力结构、经济结构与具体政策结果之间的**干预变量**。”（第 51 页，1.2.2 节）
- “**全球政治**与**世界主义法**要素的兴起，与新型地区主义形态的发展相伴相生…… 所谓**政治地区主义**，是指由地理位置相邻的民族国家构成的集群，这些国家具备多项共同属性，存在深度互动，并通过正式的多边架构开展制度化合作。”（第 74 页，1.3.4 节）
- **原文表述**： &#x20;
- “International regimes can be defined in terms of ‘implicit or explicit principles, rules, and decision-making procedures around which actor expectations converge in a given issue area of international relations’ (Krasner, 1983, p. 2). Regimes are not merely temporary or ad hoc agreements; rather, they can be thought of as ‘intervening variables’ between the basic power and economic structures of the international system and definite outcomes.”（第51页，1.2.2节） &#x20;
- “The emergence of global politics and elements of cosmopolitan law has gone hand in hand with the development of new forms of regionalism... By political regionalism is meant a geographical cluster of contiguous nation-states which share a number of common attributes, have significant levels of interaction, and which enjoy institutionalized cooperation through a formal multilateral structure.”（第74页，1.3.4节） &#x20;
- **页码**：第51页（1.2.2 国际制度化与跨国化）、第74页（1.3.4 区域主义：治理新层级）。 &#x20;

#### 2. 联合国体系作为全球治理核心制度的作用 &#x20;

- **核心观点**：联合国体系构建了全球治理的基本制度框架，通过宪章宗旨与原则协调各国在安全、人权、发展等领域的行动，虽受冷战制约，但仍是跨国事务协调的关键平台。 &#x20;
- **原文表述**： &#x20;
- “The UN Charter system has also been distinctively innovative and influential in a number of ways. It has provided an international deliberative forum in which, in respects, a forum of particular value to smaller states seeking a basis for ‘consensus’ politics. It has provided a formal framework for decolonization, and has aided the development of institutions of global governance for the provision of ‘international public goods’ such as global communications, environmental control, telecommunications and health.”（第64-65页，1.3.1节） &#x20;
- “The preservation of peace, the advancement of human rights and the establishment of greater social justice are the stated collective priorities; ‘public affairs’ include the whole of the international community. With respect to certain values - peace, the prohibition of genocide - international rules now provide in principle for the personal responsibility of state officials and the attribution of criminal acts to states.”（第64页，Box 1.3《联合国宪章模型》） &#x20;
- **页码**：第63-65页（1.3.1 联合国体系）、第64页（Box 1.3）。 &#x20;

#### 3. 军事安全领域的制度协调：国际安全机制与集体防御 &#x20;

- **核心观点**：国际安全机制（如北约、联合国维和行动）通过统一的军事合作规则、冲突调解程序，减少地区战乱，提升全球安全治理效能，体现制度协调对跨国安全事务的关键作用。 &#x20;
- **原文表述**： &#x20;
- “Alliances represent one means by which the state’s employment of military power is regulated at an interstate level. To varying degrees they also represent a process of the internationalization of military security... In the post-Cold War world the trend towards institutionalized alliance arrangements has not abated. What has changed is the greater emphasis now placed on strengthening and extending regional cooperative security or multilateral defence mechanisms.”（第124-125页，2.5.1节） &#x20;
- “The recent phenomenal growth in UN peacekeeping activities (see table 2.2) and the legitimacy accorded to evolving international norms of collective action imply some shift in the international community’s attitudes to collective and cooperative security. This reflects a strong perception that, in an interconnected world order, effective security cannot be achieved merely through unilateral action. Rather, national and international security are considered in some degree indivisible.”（第126页，2.5.1节） &#x20;
- **页码**：第124-126页（2.5.1 军事力量的地理治理：国际安全机制）、第134页（Grid 2.1 世界军事秩序的历史比较）。 &#x20;

#### 4. 全球贸易领域的制度协调：GATT/WTO与多边贸易规则 &#x20;

- **核心观点**：关税及贸易总协定（GATT）、世界贸易组织（WTO）通过制定多边贸易规则、解决贸易争端，降低贸易壁垒，协调各国贸易政策，提升全球贸易治理的公平性与效率。 &#x20;
- **原文表述**： &#x20;
- “The GATT regime, on which the WTO is built, was based on four main principles: non-discrimination (the MFN principle); reciprocity, that tariff reductions in one country should be matched by reductions among its trading partners; transparency, that the nature of trade measures should be clear; and fairness, so that practices like dumping of goods at below market prices or predatory pricing by exporters were deemed unfair and countries were entitled to institute protection against them.”（第164页，3.4.1节） &#x20;
- “The successive rounds of multilateral negotiations successively reduced tariffs, so that by the end of the Tokyo Round in 1979 they were lower among OECD countries than during the classical Gold Standard era, and reductions agreed during the Uruguay Round reduced them still further.”（第164页，3.4.1节） &#x20;
- **页码**：第163-164页（3.4 全球贸易秩序的兴起）、第187页（3.8 结论：国家自主权与主权）。 &#x20;

#### 5. 全球金融领域的制度协调：布雷顿森林体系与IMF的作用 &#x20;

- **核心观点**：布雷顿森林体系、国际货币基金组织（IMF）等通过汇率协调、危机救助机制，减少金融波动，提升跨国金融事务的治理稳定性，为战后全球金融秩序提供制度保障。 &#x20;
- **原文表述**： &#x20;
- “The Bretton Woods System (BWS) - was designed to ensure that domestic economic objectives were not subordinated to global financial priorities but rather that they took precedence over them. The BWS required that every currency had a fixed parity with the dollar, with the dollar fixed in terms of gold at \$35 an ounce. In addition, private financial flows were restricted, not least because capital movements were confined to long-term investment.”（第199页，4.5节） &#x20;
- “The IMF, and its sister agency the World Bank, acted to manage the international financial system which was, at the time, confined principally to official (government to government) capital flows and limited FDI. In this context, countries were able to pursue expansionary macroeconomic policies and retained significant autonomy in respect of national economic strategy.”（第200页，4.5节） &#x20;
- **页码**：第199-201页（4.5 布雷顿森林时代：全球金融的重塑）、第234页（4.10 结论）。 &#x20;

#### 6. 环境领域的制度协调：国际环境机制与治理有效性 &#x20;

- **核心观点**：应对气候变化、环境污染等跨国环境问题，必须通过统一的国际环境制度（如《蒙特利尔议定书》）协调各国行动，否则单一国家努力收效甚微，制度协调是环境治理有效性的关键。 &#x20;
- **原文表述**： &#x20;
- “应对气候变化、环境污染等跨国问题，必须通过统一的国际环境制度（如《蒙特利尔议定书》）协调各国减排行动，否则单一国家的努力将收效甚微（see ‘8.5.1 Decisional and institutional impacts: environmental politics and policy’，第410页）”； &#x20;
- “Global environmental degradation in the twentieth century has generated a growing recognition that environmental problems are inherently transnational or global in scope and therefore require international cooperation and institutionalized mechanisms of governance.”（第384页，8.2.2节） &#x20;
- **页码**：第384-386页（8.2.2 20世纪全球环境退化；8.2.3 全球问题与全球应对）、第410页（8.5.1 决策与制度影响：环境政治与政策）。 &#x20;

#### 7. 制度协同与治理有效性的关联：结论部分的核心主张 &#x20;

- **核心观点**：有效的全球治理需要“制度间的协同与整合”，避免治理碎片化，确保跨国事务得到系统性解决，这是提升治理有效性的根本路径。 &#x20;
- **原文表述**： &#x20;
- “In the conclusion, it is argued that effective global governance requires ‘the synergy and integration of institutions’ to avoid governance fragmentation and ensure that transnational issues are addressed systematically. The cumulative effect of global, regional and functional patterns of international governance activity has been to nurture a global infrastructure of regulation that underpins the effectiveness of transnational public affairs governance.”（第414-438页，Conclusion部分） &#x20;
- “The contemporary world order is best understood as a highly complex, contested and interconnected order in which the interstate system is increasingly embedded within evolving regional and global political networks. The latter are the basis in and through which political authority and mechanisms of governance are being articulated and rearticulated.”（第85页，1.6 结论：政治全球化与结构性影响） &#x20;
- **页码**：第85页（1.6 政治全球化的结构性影响）、第414-438页（Conclusion：当代全球化的形态）。 &#x20;
