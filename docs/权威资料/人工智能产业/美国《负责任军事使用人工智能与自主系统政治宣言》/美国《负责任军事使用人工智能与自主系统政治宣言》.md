# 美国《负责任军事使用人工智能与自主系统政治宣言》

## 军备控制与防扩散局

《负责任军事使用人工智能与自主系统政治宣言》搭建了一套规范框架，用以规制军事领域内人工智能与自主系统能力的运用。该宣言于 2023 年 2 月在荷兰海牙举行的 “军事领域负责任人工智能峰会”（2023 年海牙峰会）上正式发布，旨在凝聚国际社会对负责任行为的共识，为各国研发、部署和使用军事人工智能提供指引。同时，宣言为各国交流最佳实践、提升相关能力奠定了基础，助力签署国分享经验与理念。

2024 年 3 月 19 日至 20 日，美国国务院协同国防部，主持召开了该宣言签署国首届全体会议。此次会议是携手各签署国，推动这一开创性倡议落地实施的第一步。美国将推动签署国之间建立常态化对话机制，进一步凝聚国际社会的支持力量，促进各项负责任举措的落实。

美国鼓励世界各国支持本宣言，与其他签署国一道，共同推动人工智能与自主系统的负责任军事应用。

# Political Declaration on Responsible Military Use of Artificial Intelligence and Autonomy

[Bureau of Arms Control and Nonproliferation](https://www.state.gov/bureaus-offices/under-secretary-for-arms-control-and-international-security-affairs/bureau-of-arms-control-and-nonproliferation/ "Bureau of Arms Control and Nonproliferation")

The Political Declaration on Responsible Military Use of Artificial Intelligence and Autonomy provides a normative framework addressing the use of these capabilities in the military domain.  Launched in February 2023 at the Responsible AI in the Military Domain Summit (REAIM 2023) in the Hague, the Declaration aims to build international consensus around responsible behavior and guide states’ development, deployment, and use of military AI.  The Declaration provides a basis for exchanging best practices and building states’ capacities, which will allow endorsing States to share experience and ideas. 

The Department of State, in collaboration with the Department of Defense, convened the inaugural plenary meeting of States endorsing the Political Declaration on March 19-20, 2024.  This plenary meeting was the first step to working with endorsing States to operationalize and implement this groundbreaking initiative.  The United States will convene regular dialogue among endorsing States to further promote international support for and implementation of these responsible practices.

The United States encourages all states to support the Declaration and join with other endorsing States to promote responsible military use of AI and autonomy.
