    Mokyr J.The lever of riches：Technological creativity and economic progress[M]. Oxford：Oxford University Press, 1990.

    Joel Mokyr（乔尔·莫基尔）是当代最具影响力的经济史学家之一，长期任教于美国西北大学，以研究技术进步、知识演化与长期经济发展著称。他的学术贡献在于，将技术创新置于更宽广的历史与制度背景中加以分析，系统揭示了技术创造力如何通过知识积累、制度环境和社会选择机制，塑造不同国家和地区的长期发展轨迹。Mokyr被普遍视为将经济史与现代增长理论有机结合的代表性学者。

    Mokyr（1990）从经济史视角系统分析了技术创造力在长期经济发展中的核心作用，指出持续的技术进步通过重塑生产方式和经济结构，深刻影响国家发展轨迹，为理解技术进步与经济增长之间的长期关系提供了重要历史依据。

    《财富的杠杆：技术创造力与经济进步》主要观点及原文摘录（含页码标注） ¶

    一、核心论点：技术创造力是西方财富增长的核心杠杆 ¶

    1.1 技术创造力的本质与价值 ¶

    • 原文：“Technological creativity was at the very base of the rise of the West. It was the lever of its riches.”（Preface，无具体页码，前言部分核心观点）
    • 原文：“Technological progress has been one of the most potent forces in history in that it has provided society with what economists call a 'free lunch,' that is, an increase in output that is not commensurate with the increase in effort and cost necessary to bring it about.”（Chapter 1，p.3）
    • 解读：技术创造力是西方崛起的根本基础，其核心价值在于能产生“免费午餐”——以更低的努力和成本实现更高产出，这是西方摆脱贫困、实现财富增长的关键。

    1.2 技术创造力与科学、艺术创造力的差异 ¶

    • 原文：“Technological creativity is quite different from artistic or scientific creativity. It tends to be more down-to-earth, with such mundane characteristics as dexterity and greed at the center of the act. Yet it does share with the arts and the sciences its occasional dependence on inspiration, luck, serendipity, genius, and the unexplained drive of people to go somewhere where none has gone before.”（Preface，无具体页码）
    • 解读：技术创造力更注重实用性，以灵巧、逐利为核心，但也与艺术、科学创造力共享灵感、运气、天赋等要素，区别于纯粹的理论探索或艺术表达。

    二、经济增长的四种类型划分 ¶

    2.1 索洛型增长（Solovian growth）——资本积累驱动 ¶

    • 原文：“Investment (increases in the capital stock). The productivity of labor, and with it the average standard of living, depends on the quantities and qualities of the equipment and tools an average worker has at his or her disposal (known among economists as the capital-labor ratio). When capital accumulates at a rate faster than the growth of the labor force, so that each worker has increasingly more capital to work with, economic growth will take place.”（Chapter 1，p.4）
    • 解读：依赖资本存量增长，通过提高“资本-劳动比”提升劳动生产率，但需以储蓄（牺牲当前消费）为代价，无“免费午餐”属性。

    2.2 斯密型增长（Smithian growth）——商业扩张驱动 ¶

    • 原文：“Commercial expansion. It is the standard fare of intermediate courses in microeconomics to illustrate that an increase in the exchange of goods, services, labor, or capital can be beneficial to all partners involved... These gains are known as the gains from trade and represent a good example of a free lunch. Trade, as Adam Smith pointed out in 1776, leads to a growth in the Wealth of Nations.”（Chapter 1，p.5）
    • 解读：通过贸易扩张和分工深化实现增长，核心是“贸易收益”，属于“免费午餐”，但依赖交易成本降低和产权保护，并非技术驱动。

    2.3 规模效应增长（Scale or size effects）——人口规模驱动 ¶

    • 原文：“It is sometimes maintained that population growth itself can lead to per capita income growth... For very small populations growth in numbers alone would make specialization possible and lead to gains in output. Moreover, at least up to some point there are fixed costs and indivisibilities... that can be deployed effectively only for relatively large populations.”（Chapter 1，p.6）
    • 解读：人口增长通过扩大市场规模、实现专业化分工和分摊固定成本促进增长，但超过临界点后会因资源约束陷入边际收益递减。

    2.4 熊彼特型增长（Schumpeterian growth）——技术与知识驱动 ¶

    • 原文：“Increases in the stock of human knowledge, which includes technological progress proper, as well as changes in institutions... Schumpeterian growth as 'capitalist expansion deriving from continuous, though fluctuating, technological change and innovation, financed by the extension of credit.' This book is indeed about little else but technological change and innovation.”（Chapter 1，p.7）
    • 解读：核心是技术变革与创新，涵盖知识存量增长和制度调整，不局限于资本主义或信贷融资，是长期经济增长的核心动力，也是本书重点分析对象。

    三、技术变革的关键特征：微观发明与宏观发明的互补性 ¶

    3.1 微观发明（Microinventions）的定义与作用 ¶

    • 原文：“Microinventions as the small, incremental steps that improve, adapt, and streamline existing techniques already in use, reducing costs, improving form and function, increasing durability, and reducing energy and raw material requirements.”（Chapter 1，p.12）
    • 解读：对现有技术的小幅增量改进，如降低成本、优化功能等，数量多、频率高，是生产率提升的主要来源。

    3.2 宏观发明（Macroinventions）的定义与作用 ¶

    • 原文：“Macroinventions, on the other hand, are those inventions in which a radical new idea, without clear precedent, emerges more or less ab nihilo... Without novel and radical departures, the continuous process of improving and refining existing techniques would run into diminishing returns and eventually peter out.”（Chapter 1，p.12）
    • 解读：无明确先例的突破性发明（如印刷机、蒸汽机），虽数量少，但能打破技术瓶颈，为微观发明提供新方向，避免技术进步陷入边际收益递减。

    3.3 二者的互补关系 ¶

    • 原文：“The essential feature of technological progress is that the macroinventions and microinventions are not substitutes but complements. Without subsequent microinventions, most macroinventions would end up as curiosa in musea or sketchbooks... Without novel and radical departures, the continuous process of improving and refining existing techniques would run into diminishing returns and eventually peter out.”（Chapter 1，p.12）
    • 解读：宏观发明需微观发明完善才能落地，微观发明需宏观发明开辟新领域，二者共同构成技术进步的完整链条。

    四、不同历史时期的技术发展对比 ¶

    4.1 古典时代（Classical Antiquity，约公元前500-公元500年）：技术潜力未充分释放 ¶

    • 原文：“Classical society was inventive, original, and inquisitive. But it was not particularly technologically creative. It built waterwheels, but it did not really exploit waterpower. It constructed fore-and-aft rigged ships, but only on a small scale. It had a good understanding of glassmaking, it was a comparatively literate society, and it realized that light rays could be manipulated, yet it never produced spectacles.”（Chapter 2，p.28）
    • 解读：古典文明（希腊、罗马）在公共工程（如道路、水道）和机械理论（如杠杆、齿轮）上有成就，但技术应用集中于公共部门，私人部门（农业、制造业）技术进步缓慢，且未将发明转化为经济增长动力（如未普及水力、未发明眼镜）。

    4.2 中世纪（Middle Ages，约500-1500年）：西方技术创造力的萌芽 ¶

    • 原文：“Medieval Western technology drew from three sources: classical antiquity, Islamic and Asian societies, and its own original creativity... Unlike their Roman ancestors, medieval men and women were surrounded by water-driven machines doing the more arduous work for them. The waterwheel may not have been invented in medieval Europe, but it was there that its use spread far beyond anything seen in earlier times.”（Chapter 3，p.32-33）

    • 关键技术成就（Chapter 3，p.33-45）：

    • 农业技术：重犁（carruca）、三圃制（three-field system）、马轭（horse collar）；

    • 能源技术：水车（尤其是上击式水车）、风车；
    • 交通技术：维京船、柯克船（cog）、尾舵（sternpost rudder）；

    • 机械技术：机械钟（weight-driven clock）、印刷机（Gutenberg movable type）。

    • 解读：中世纪虽被视为“黑暗时代”，但通过融合多文明技术、原创发明，实现了技术突破，尤其在农业和能源领域，为后续西方技术领先奠定基础。

    4.3 工业革命时期（1750-1830年）：技术革命的爆发 ¶

    • 原文：“The Industrial Revolution was not primarily a macroeconomic event that led to a sudden acceleration of the rate of growth... It is appropriate to think about the Industrial Revolution primarily in terms of accelerating and unprecedented technological change... a string of novel ideas and insights that made it possible to produce more and better goods and do so more efficiently.”（Chapter 5，p.81）

    • 核心技术突破（Chapter 5，p.82-105）：

    • 动力技术：瓦特蒸汽机（Watt's steam engine，分离式冷凝器）、特里维西克高压蒸汽机（high-pressure engine）；

    • 冶金技术：达比焦炭炼铁（Abraham Darby's coke smelting）、科特搅炼法（Henry Cort's puddling and rolling process）；

    • 纺织技术：阿克莱特水力纺纱机（Arkwright's water frame）、哈格里夫斯珍妮纺纱机（Hargreaves's spinning jenny）、克朗普顿骡机（Crompton's mule）。

    • 解读：工业革命的核心是技术变革的加速，通过宏观发明（如蒸汽机）与微观发明（如纺纱机改进）的结合，实现了生产效率的爆发式增长，奠定西方经济霸权基础。

    五、技术进步的影响因素分析 ¶

    5.1 制度与产权保护 ¶

    • 原文：“The definition of property rights on new technology constitutes another condition for innovators to cash in on technological progress... Patents, monopolies, grants, pensions, prizes, and medals have provided would-be innovators with the rewards deemed necessary to keep up a high level of inventive activity.”（Chapter 7，p.158）
    • 案例（Chapter 10，p.242）：英国1624年《垄断法案》（Statute of Monopolies）确立专利制度，为瓦特、阿克莱特等发明家提供激励，而欧洲大陆专利制度滞后（法国1791年、德国19世纪初才建立），导致技术应用滞后。

    5.2 科学与技术的互动 ¶

    • 原文：“Before 1850, it seems, most important inventions... were of the empirical kind, and the role of metatechnological knowledge was marginal. But appearances can be misleading... Galileo's theory of mechanics was of great importance to all subsequent developments in machine design.”（Chapter 7，p.165）
    • 解读：1850年前技术进步以经验为主，但科学（如伽利略力学）为技术提供理论基础；1850年后科学对技术的作用增强（如化学对染料工业的推动），但经验性发明仍未消失。

    5.3 文化与价值观 ¶

    • 原文：“Western Christianity, as it eventually evolved, reinforced these tendencies... The belief in a rational and calculating God, the designer of a huge intricate mechanism called the Creation, who 'commands man to rule the world and to help to fulfill the divine will in it as a creative cooperator with him.'”（Chapter 8，p.195）
    • 解读：西方基督教的“人类支配自然”价值观，区别于东方“人与自然和谐”理念，为技术创新提供文化动力，促使人们主动改造自然以提升物质生活。

    5.4 政治与竞争环境 ¶

    • 原文：“Europe was politically fragmented between more or less autonomous units that competed for survival, wealth, and power... The struggle for survival guaranteed that in the long run rulers could not afford to be hostile to changes that increased the economic power of their realm because of the real danger that an innovation or innovator would emigrate to benefit a rival.”（Chapter 8，p.200）
    • 解读：欧洲政治分裂形成竞争环境，各国为生存不得不接纳技术创新（如英国吸引欧洲大陆发明家），而中国、伊斯兰帝国等统一政权易因保守倾向压制技术进步。

    六、关键对比案例：中国与欧洲的技术发展差异 ¶

    6.1 中国的技术优势与停滞 ¶

    • 原文：“In the centuries before 1400, the Chinese developed an amazing technological momentum... Many of their innovations eventually found their way to Europe... Yet China failed to become what Europe eventually became. At about the time we associate with the beginning of the Renaissance in Europe, China's technological progress slowed down, and ultimately came to a full stop.”（Chapter 9，p.210-211）
    • 中国技术成就（Chapter 9，p.211-215）：铸铁技术（公元前200年）、造纸术（公元100年左右）、印刷术（1045年毕昇活字）、指南针（公元960年左右）、水车与纺织机械。

    • 停滞原因（Chapter 9，p.218-225）：

    • 政治因素：明朝（1368-1644）后中央集权强化，禁止远洋航行（如郑和下西洋后停航），压制私人创新；

    • 文化因素：儒家思想重“和谐”轻“改造自然”，缺乏西方“支配自然”的价值观；
    • 经济因素：人口增长导致劳动密集型技术依赖，缺乏技术替代动力（如未发展机械纺纱）。

    6.2 欧洲的技术追赶与超越 ¶

    • 原文：“Europeans appreciated useful knowledge regardless of the source; Asian cultures, with the exception of nineteenth-century Japan, did not... When Europeans were exposed to new information, their sense of wonderment was soon replaced by the thought of how to exploit the new knowledge.”（Chapter 9，p.223）
    • 解读：欧洲通过吸收中国、伊斯兰技术（如造纸术、指南针），结合自身竞争环境与文化价值观，实现技术追赶；1500年后通过航海探索、科学革命和工业革命，最终超越中国，成为技术领导者。
